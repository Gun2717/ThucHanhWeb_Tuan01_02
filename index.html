One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyert by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re- evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets,and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and intersting. Other books by John Grisham include The Film, The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
